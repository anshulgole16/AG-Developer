## Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme:
- Primary: Purple (#8B5CF6)
- Dark Background: Near Black (#0F0F0F)
- Surface: Dark Gray (#1A1A1A)
- Text Primary: White
- Text Secondary: Gray

### Typography:
- Headings: Bold, Sans-serif
- Body: Clean, readable
- Use simple English
- Short sentences

### Spacing:
- Good padding between sections (py-24)
- Card gaps: 6 (gap-6)

### Mobile Responsive:
- Stack cards on mobile
- Hamburger menu for navigation

### Hover Effects:
- Cards: slight lift (-translate-y-1)
- Buttons: scale increase
- Links: color change

### Copy Style:
- Simple English
- Focus on results, not skills
- Action-oriented words
- No generic phrases
